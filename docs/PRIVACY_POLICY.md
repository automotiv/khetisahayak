# Privacy Policy for Kheti Sahayak

**Last Updated: January 7, 2026**

## Introduction

Kheti Sahayak ("Farm Helper") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Camera Access
We request camera access to enable crop disease detection. Images captured are:
- Processed locally on your device or sent to our secure servers for AI analysis
- Not shared with third parties
- Deleted after analysis unless you choose to save them

### Location Data
We collect location data to provide:
- Accurate weather forecasts for your area
- Location-specific agricultural recommendations
- Nearby marketplace listings

### Personal Information
When you create an account, we may collect:
- Name
- Phone number
- Email address
- Farm location and size

## How We Use Your Information

- Provide crop disease diagnosis
- Deliver personalized weather alerts
- Connect you with agricultural experts
- Improve our AI models (anonymized data only)
- Send relevant notifications about farming activities

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure server infrastructure
- Regular security audits

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:
- Agricultural experts (only when you request consultation)
- Service providers who assist our operations
- Legal authorities when required by law

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request data deletion
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Update your information

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
