## Play Console Submission Steps

1. **Open Play Console** → Select App → Policy → Content Rating

2. **Start New Questionnaire** or **Edit Existing**

3. **Select Category:** 
   - Primary: **Utility**
   - Secondary: **Education**

4. **Answer Questions** using this document as reference

5. **Review Summary** before submitting

6. **Submit for Rating**

---

## Content Rating Certificate

After submission, you'll receive:
- IARC rating certificate
- Rating icons for all regions
- Content descriptors (if any)

**Download and save the certificate for records.**
