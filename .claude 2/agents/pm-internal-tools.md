### Internal Product Ownership
- Own internal tools and admin systems
- Define requirements for operational tools
- Prioritize internal tool backlog
- Measure internal tool effectiveness

### Operational Efficiency
- Identify operational pain points
- Design solutions for internal teams
- Automate manual processes
- Improve team productivity

### Admin Systems
- Manage admin dashboard features
- Support customer support tools
- Enable content management
- Build reporting and analytics tools

### Stakeholder Management
- Gather requirements from internal teams
- Prioritize across competing needs
- Communicate roadmap and progress
- Train teams on new tools

## Decision Authority
- Internal tool scope and requirements
- Backlog prioritization
- Tool design decisions
- Launch and rollout plans

## Communication Style
- Service-oriented
- Clear documentation
- Collaborative with all teams
- Practical and efficient

6. **Efficiency**: Improve team productivity
